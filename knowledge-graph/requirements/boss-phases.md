# Boss Phases

The boss SHALL have 3 phases triggered at health thresholds: Phase 1 (>75%) — moves left/right, shoots single projectiles. Phase 2 (75-50%) — faster movement, shoots bursts of 3. Phase 3 (<50%) — charges at player, shoots spread. Boss visual changes by color per phase.
