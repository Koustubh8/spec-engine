# Coin Collection

25 coins SHALL be placed at level generation time. Coins SHALL be collected when the player is within 20px proximity. Collected coins SHALL increment the player's coin counter. The HUD SHALL display coin count. Particles SHALL spawn on enemy death (8 red squares, random scatter). A timer SHALL track elapsed play time.
