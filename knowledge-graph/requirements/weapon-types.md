# Weapon Types

At least 3 weapons: Default (single shot, medium speed), Spread (3 shots in a fan), Rapid (fast fire, low damage). Weapon fires a projectile character: `-` for horizontal, `|` for vertical, `*` for spread. Pick up weapon power-ups to switch.
